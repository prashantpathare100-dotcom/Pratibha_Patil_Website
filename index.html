Frequently Asked Questions

When was Pratibha Patil born?

December 19, 1934 in Nadgaon, Maharashtra.

How long was she President?

From 2007 to 2012 (5 years).

What were her major initiatives?

Women's empowerment, education, healthcare.

Which positions did she hold before presidency?

MLA, Deputy Minister, Rajya Sabha MP, Governor of Rajasthan.
