Life Timeline

Key milestones in the journey of India's first female President

1934

Birth in Nadgaon

Born on December 19 in Nadgaon village, Jalgaon district, Maharashtra.

1962

First Election Victory

Elected to the Maharashtra Legislative Assembly for the first time.

1967-1972

Deputy Minister

Served as Deputy Minister in various departments including Education and Health.

1986

Rajya Sabha Member

Elected to the Rajya Sabha, the upper house of Indian Parliament.

2004

Governor of Rajasthan

Appointed as the Governor of Rajasthan, the first woman to hold that position.

2007

President of India

Elected as the 12th President of India, becoming the first woman to hold the office.

2012

End of Presidential Term

Completed her five-year term as President, continuing her social work.

Pratibha Patil

First Female President of India (2007-2012). A life dedicated to public service, social justice, and empowering women.
